About the Clinic

Purpose

A completely free basketball clinic for youth to learn fundamentals, teamwork, and skills from NBA champions and Lakers legends.

Who Can Attend

Young athletes ages 8-18, coaches, and basketball enthusiasts of all skill levels. No experience necessary!

What You'll Learn

Professional-level drills, game strategy, skill development, teamwork, and the championship mindset from Lakers stars.
